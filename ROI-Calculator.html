What is this ROI Calculator?

This ROI Calculator helps crypto traders calculate return on investment for trades. Combine it with Take Profit Calculator, Fees Calculator, and Position Size Calculator for full trade evaluation.

How to Use This Calculator

ROI Calculator Example

If your entry price is $100, exit price $120, and investment $1000:

ROI = (Exit – Entry) ÷ Entry × 100% = (120–100)/100 × 100% = 20%

Your profit = Investment × ROI = $1000 × 20% = $200.

Instructions & FAQ

1. What is ROI?

Return on Investment (ROI) measures percentage profit or loss relative to invested capital.

2. How to calculate ROI?

Use (Exit Price – Entry Price) ÷ Entry Price × 100%.

3. Can I include fees?

Fees are not included; use Fees Calculator to adjust net ROI.

4. Can I combine with Take Profit?

Yes, Take Profit Calculator defines target prices.

5. Suitable for all crypto assets?

Yes, works for any crypto trade.

6. Beginner friendly?

Yes, simple calculations to evaluate trades.

7. Can I calculate profit in USD?

Yes, multiply investment amount by ROI.

8. Can I use leverage?

ROI is independent of leverage, but margin affects absolute gain/loss.

9. Why track ROI?

To evaluate trading performance and optimize portfolio.

10. Recommended workflow?

Plan trades with Stop Loss, Take Profit, Fees, and Position Size for accurate ROI.
