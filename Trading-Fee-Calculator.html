How to Use This Calculator

Trading Fee Example

If trade amount = $1000, maker fee = 0.1%, taker fee = 0.2%:

Maker Fee = 1000 × 0.1% = $1

Taker Fee = 1000 × 0.2% = $2

Total Fees = $3
